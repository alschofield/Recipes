# Third-Party Design/UX Brief (V1)

Use this brief when engaging a UI/UX specialist or contracted product designer.

## Objective

Raise Ingrediential web + native UX quality to a modern, reliable launch standard and eliminate high-friction behaviors.

## Product Surfaces in Scope

- Web: home, discover, detail, auth, favorites, account.
- Mobile: Discover, Saved, Profile tabs on Android and iOS.

## Required Outcomes

1. UX audit findings with severity ranking (P0/P1/P2).
2. Prioritized redesign recommendations for hierarchy, states, and interaction clarity.
3. Updated component/token guidance (web + mobile parity intent where applicable).
4. Acceptance criteria and QA checks for launch readiness.

## Known Focus Areas

- Empty/error/retry state clarity.
- Discover/detail scanability and card rhythm.
- Auth/session clarity in account/profile flows.
- Offline queue and sync-status comprehension.
- Accessibility and dynamic text behavior.

# Third-Party Legal Review Brief (V1)

Use this brief when engaging external legal/privacy/compliance counsel.

## Objective

Reduce launch risk for privacy, licensing, and store compliance before V1 release.

## Required Scope

1. Privacy policy and disclosure review
   - web + mobile behavior alignment
   - voice input and telemetry language accuracy
2. Store compliance review
   - Play Data Safety
   - App Store privacy declarations
   - wording consistency with policies
3. LLM dataset/compliance review
   - training dataset legal status and attribution obligations
   - unresolved lanes listed in LLM checklist
4. OSS attribution review
   - runtime dependency attribution and notice requirements

## Desired Deliverables

- Written risk memo with launch blockers/non-blockers.
- Required policy/declaration edits.
- Attribution/notice obligations checklist.
- Final approval letter or signoff note.

## Artifacts to Share

- `docs/archive/deliverables/legal-compliance-pre-audit-report-2026-03-23.md`
- `docs/mobile/store-submission-pack-template-v1.md`
- `docs/mobile/voice-privacy-policy-v1.md`
- `llm/CHECKLIST.md`
- `docs/ops/v1-external-inputs-checklist.md`

## Engagement Model

- Recommended: fixed-scope review package with one revision pass.
- Timebox: 3-7 business days for first review cycle.
